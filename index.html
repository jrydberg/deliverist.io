Deliverist

The build, measure and learn loop for your code and process.

Continuous Delivery
... and continuous improvement

Deliverist is not your regular continuous integration or continuous delivery service. We also work hard at making you the best developer you can be.

Our vision is that we're the helping hand that makes you faster and stronger than the rest. Our goal is to be your competitive advantage.

Actionable feedback on your code

Deliverist want you to deliver quality changes at a stunning pace. For that to happen you need actionable and high quality feedback.

If your change is too big? We'll let you know. If you're modifying code that has had bugs in the past, we'll make sure you pay more attention. And we keep track of quality metrics for you.

Keep track of your production environment

You should know what goes in and out of your production environment.

We'll keep track of all your deployments and which changes were included. We also integrate with other services like PagerDuty so you can quickly notice if a deployment resulted in a alert of some sort.

We give you stars!

We're not successful if you're not successful, therefore we're gonna do our best to make you sure you follow best practices around code quality and delivery processes.

If you don't, we'll tell you what you need to do to improve.

If you do, you get a gold star.
